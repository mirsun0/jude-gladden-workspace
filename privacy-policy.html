# Privacy Policy **Effective Date: July 6, 2026** This Privacy Policy outlines how personal data is handled by our private digital notebook and SMS-based automated capture system. ### 1. Information Collected and Scope This system is an exclusively private utility designed for personal workflow organization, rapid note-taking, and time-management reminders. The only data collected, processed, or stored is the mobile phone number and the text-based message content voluntarily transmitted by the primary user for note archiving. ### 2. Strict Non-Sharing of Mobile Data Your privacy is treated with absolute confidentiality. Mobile information, phone numbers, or text content collected for the purpose of SMS ingest, note logging, or task synchronization will absolutely never be shared, sold, rented, or traded to any third parties, affiliates, or partners for marketing, promotional, or commercial purposes under any circumstances. ### 3. Data Storage, Hosting, and Security All incoming text communications are safely routed and stored locally on private, self-hosted network-attached storage (NAS) architecture. We utilize secure local networks and industry-standard encryption protocols to maintain complete local data ownership and prevent unauthorized access. ### 4. Message Frequency Message frequency is entirely user-dependent. Outbound text messages from this system are strictly non-promotional and will only be sent to your device in direct response to an active, user-initiated request—such as a scheduled task notification, log confirmation, or system alert. ### 5. Changes to This Policy We reserve the right to modify this operational privacy policy. Any changes to data handling protocols will be published instantly to this public URL.
